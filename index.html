10 interesting facts about the Moon

The Moon has only been walked on by 12 people

The Moon has only been walked on by 12 people; all American males.

The Moon has no atmosphere

This means that the surface of the Moon is unprotected from cosmic rays and meteorites.

The Moon has quakes

Moonquakes are caused by the gravitational pull of the Earth.

The first spacecraft to reach the Moon

The first spacecraft to reach the Moon was Luna 1 in 1959.

The Moon will be visited again

NASA plans to return astronauts to the Moon in the near future.
